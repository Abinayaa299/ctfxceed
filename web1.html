      About XCEED


“Distance never stopped goals”
Xceed, the forerunner of kurukshetra excellently performs the duty of crusading to carry extravagantly the pride of kurukshetra nationwide. We mainly focus on outstretching to the other side of the country consisting of students and corporate companies to guide standard participation to kurukshetra. Xceed conducts many fascinating events and marvelous workshops organized by the enthusiastic team of CEG Tech Forum. The winners gain a pathway to showcase their caliber and qualities at the finale stage of kurukshetra. ...


OBJECTIVES

  • NATIONAL

       Xceed being the precursor of Kurukshetra is conducted in various colleges across the country. The bot building, coding & technical events are the tip of the ice berg of the spectacle that is to occur . These events test the technical, analytical, statistical and coding skills of the participants.

       Xceed also features an extensive line-up of interactive work-shops that help students enrich their technical and managerial skills and also nurture an individual’s holistic development.


  • CORPORATE

       Corporate XCEED is the latest brain child of the XCEED team to explore new uncharted lands pushing the boundaries of Kurukshetra. This novel pursuit features general, technical and managerial events being conducted in various corporate pavilions across the city. Kurukshetra is the only tech-fest to conduct events among corporates grade symbolizing its excellent corporate relationship and network. It provides an equal opportunity to both the students and corporates to benefit mutually from their knowledge and experience.
